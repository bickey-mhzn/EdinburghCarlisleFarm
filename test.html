RESTAURANT & BAR

EST 1979

Cafe Gandolfi is one of Glasgow's oldest family-run restaurants. It is renowned as a Scottish institution, offering the best of Scottish produce in dishes which evolve and delight in equal measure. The importance of the all-round dining experience is underpinned by a culture and ethos among staff, management and owner which embrace warmth, welcome and creativity. That creativity is further enhanced by its patronage of artists and artisans and its global reputation for housing one of the best collections of Tim Stead furniture.

OUR STORY

DEAGH BHIADH, DEAGH BHEANNACHD

Well fed, well blessed. The Gaelic proverb displayed on the wall of the Cafe Gandolfi is my aspiration for everybody who eats here. Its simple yet deeply ambitious, an honest proposition that draws on my hertiage and one thats steeped in the values of the Hebrides, where good food is one of lifes atavisic joys and where eating together strengthens the bonds between family and friends in a way thats now rare, as we increasingly make do with grabbing food on the run, depriving ourselves of the pleasure of valuable, shared moments.

- Seamas MacInnes
