Edinburgh and Carlisle Farm & Petting Zoo offers a charming countryside escape where families can enjoy hands on encounters with friendly animals and learn about life on a working farm. Conveniently located between the historic cities of Edinburgh and Carlisle, the farm features wide green pastures, peaceful walking areas, and a welcoming petting zoo filled with goats, rabbits, alpacas, and other gentle creatures. Visitors can interact with the animals, explore seasonal farm activities, and even take home fresh local produce from the small farm shop. It’s a perfect destination for a relaxed day out, blending education, nature, and wholesome fun for all ages.

Beyond its day‑to‑day attractions, Edinburgh and Carlisle Farm & Petting Zoo offers a rich and ever‑changing program of seasonal activities that allow visitors to experience the rhythms of farm life throughout the year. During spring, guests can witness the excitement of new life on the farm, enjoy guided walks, and learn about caring for young animals. Summer brings picnic‑friendly fields, open play areas, and longer days perfect for exploring nature trails or joining outdoor family workshops. As autumn arrives, the farm highlights harvest traditions, with opportunities to discover how crops are grown, take part in themed events, and enjoy the vibrant countryside scenery. Even in the winter months, the farm provides a cosy respite with sheltered viewing areas, educational talks, and opportunities to appreciate the calm beauty of the rural landscape. With every season offering something unique, the farm invites visitors to return again and again for new experiences and lasting memories.
