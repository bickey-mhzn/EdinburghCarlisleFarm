OUR STORY

At Edinburgh and Carlisle Farm & Petting Zoo, everything began with a simple idea: create a place where nature, animals, and people come together in a meaningful and memorable way. What started as a small family-run farm has grown into a lively countryside destination where visitors of all ages can experience the joy of rural life.

Surrounded by rolling green fields and peaceful walking paths, the farm was originally home to a few goats, sheep, and rabbits cared for by a group of passionate keepers who loved sharing their knowledge with the community. What began as informal animal encounters soon blossomed into a dedicated petting zoo—offering children and adults the chance to meet, feed, and learn about friendly farm animals in a safe and welcoming environment.

As more families visited, the farm expanded its offerings to include seasonal events, guided nature walks, wildlife ponds, farm workshops, and hands-on activities that spark curiosity and appreciation for the outdoors. Each new addition was built with the same intention: to foster connection. Connection between visitors and animals. Between families and nature. And between communities and the land that sustains them.
